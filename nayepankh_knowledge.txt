ABOUT NAYEPANKH FOUNDATION
NayePankh Foundation is a UP Government registered NGO founded on 28th March 2021. It is one of the biggest student-led organizations in India, working towards uplifting the underprivileged. The foundation is registered under the Indian Society Act 1860, affiliated with NITI Aayog, and holds 80G and 12A certifications.

HOW IT STARTED
NayePankh Foundation was started during the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020 by a group of high school students who wanted to help the underprivileged. They started by distributing food and essential supplies during the lockdown. The foundation officially launched on 28th March 2021 and has since grown into a large team of volunteers from different parts of Uttar Pradesh.

WHAT NAYEPANKH MEANS
NayePankh means "new wings" — the foundation aims to give wings to the underprivileged sections of society and uplift them for a better future. Their motto is "BADALTE BHARAT KI NAYI TASVEER" which means "A new picture of changing India."

WHAT THEY DO
NayePankh Foundation works in the following areas:
1. Food Distribution — distributing free food to underprivileged people and stray animals to fight hunger.
2. Sanitary Napkins — providing free sanitary napkins to women and creating awareness campaigns about personal hygiene among women and youth.
3. Clothes Distribution — providing clothes to poor families in need.
4. Education — educating underprivileged sectors of society for a better future.
5. Awareness Campaigns — running campaigns on personal hygiene, menstrual health, and community empowerment.

IMPACT
NayePankh Foundation has helped more than 2 lakh (200,000) underprivileged people till date. They have trained and supported more than 30,000 interns. The foundation has been featured in major newspapers including The Pioneer, Dainik Jagran, and Hindustan. They operate primarily in Kanpur and Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh.

TAX BENEFITS FOR DONORS
NayePankh Foundation is 80G and 12A certified. This means donors get 50% relief in income tax on their donations. Donations to NayePankh Foundation qualify for tax benefits under Indian law.

VISION AND MISSION
NayePankh Foundation was founded with the vision to uplift underprivileged and marginalized communities and provide them access to education, healthcare, and basic necessities. They believe every child has the right to dream and achieve their aspirations regardless of their socio-economic background. Their mission is to break the cycle of poverty and provide hope to those in need.
